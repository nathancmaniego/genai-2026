# [Project Name] - AI-Driven Mental Health Telemetry

## 📖 Overview

[Project Name] is an intelligent, continuous-care mental health platform designed to bridge the gap between clinical sessions. By synthesizing continuous biometric data (HRV, sleep, blood pressure) with NLP-driven conversational journaling, the platform acts as a proactive first-line triage system for patients while providing mental health professionals with a predictive, data-backed clinical dashboard.

## ✨ Core Features

### For Patients (Mobile Application)

- **Continuous Biometric Syncing:** Integrates directly with Apple HealthKit to continuously ingest and analyze Heart Rate Variability (HRV), sleep staging, and blood pressure.
- **Agentic AI Journaling:** A conversational interface for daily logging, venting, and reflection, powered by a fine-tuned LLM.
- **Real-Time Micro-Interventions:** Delivers immediate, evidence-based coping mechanisms (e.g., breathing exercises, grounding techniques) triggered by negative shifts in biometric or sentiment data.
- **Dynamic Escalation & Triage:** Automatically detects elevated risk states and prompts users to schedule immediate or earlier sessions with their provider.

### For Professionals (Clinical Dashboard)

- **Longitudinal Telemetry View:** Aggregates time-series biometric data and sentiment analysis into a digestible, quick-glance dashboard.
- **Automated Psychological Reporting:** Generates standardized, readable mental health summaries prior to appointments, reducing manual charting and baseline-setting.
- **Intelligent Alerting System:** Triggers push notifications to the provider for critical patient escalations (e.g., severe sleep crashes paired with depressive journal sentiment).
